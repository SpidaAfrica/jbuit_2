HERO BACKGROUND VIDEOS
======================
Drop three short, muted background clips here to power the hero carousel:

  web.mp4     — footage of websites / web apps
  mobile.mp4  — footage of mobile apps / phones
  ai.mp4      — footage of AI systems / dashboards / automation

Tips:
- Keep them short (5–10s), they loop automatically.
- Compress for web (H.264 .mp4, ~1080p, a few MB each) so the page stays fast.
- No audio needed (they play muted).
- Until you add them, the hero shows a polished animated gradient automatically,
  so the site never looks broken.

To change the filenames or add more slides, edit:
  src/components/HeroCarousel.jsx  (the SLIDES array)
